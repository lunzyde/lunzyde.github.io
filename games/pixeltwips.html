In memory of a great mind - José M. De Abóngüez


A simplistic endless runner with retro arcade aesthetics that provides different ways to customize your experience.

This game can be played by anyone looking for a challenge, as well as those who seek an easier time. You can select a preset on Casual Play or make your own in Custom Play. Try to reach the furthest Distance and Stage!
