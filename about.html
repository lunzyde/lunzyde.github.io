About

Bryan B. Bonilla Díaz is a Puerto Rican, independent developer with a diverse experience and focus on games and audio. He’s currently working on his passion project PixelTwips, which he started development in 2014 and later released in 2021. Apart from that, he is a volunteer for the Puerto Rico Game Developers Association (PRGDA).

Bryan studied game design and development at the Inter American University of Puerto Rico (Bayamón Campus), in which he was a Spokesperson for the InterDev Computer Science Student Association from 2017 to 2020. As part of it, he hosted workshops related to game development and organized the first game jam in the campus history.

He has been around the local game development scene for 5+ years, in which he is recognized as an active member within the Puerto Rico Game Developers community since 2018.
