About

Bryan B. Bonilla Díaz is a Puerto Rican, Independent Game Developer focused on games and audio. He's the Chief Advocate for the Puerto Rico Game Developers Association (PRGDA), Sound Designer for Behbes Adventure!, and the developer behind PixelTwips. He’s currently studying the Game Design M.S. at Full Sail.

Bryan graduated from the Game Design & Development B.S. at the Inter American University of Puerto Rico (Bayamón Campus), where he was a Spokesperson for the InterDev Computer Science Student Association from 2017 to 2020. As part of it, he hosted workshops related to game development and organized the first game jam in the campus history.

He has been around the Puerto Rican game development scene for 5+ years, this includes developing and prototyping games. Apart from this, he is also a self-taught bedroom producer. Bryan is recognized as an active member within the Puerto Rico Game Developers community since 2018.
